About BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement using your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. Use our BMI calculator to check your BMI and find your healthy weight range.

BMI Categories

• Underweight = <18.5
• Normal weight = 18.5–24.9
• Overweight = 25–29.9
• Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater

How to Use This Calculator

Simply adjust the sliders to match your height and weight. The calculator will instantly show your BMI and indicate whether you're in a healthy weight range. Results are shown in both metric and imperial units for convenience.
